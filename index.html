What is CovidSight ?

An analytics framework that uses cameras with Computer Vision algorithms, to automatically verify the compliance of safety rules against COVID-19, without human intervention or unreliable equipment.

About US

CovidSight is a tech startup born in 2020, focused in people and mask usage detection, safe social distance verification and body temperature verification in real-time using Machine Learning.

Mission

To provide accurate body temperature, safe social distance and mask usage verifications in real-time to enforce the new health regulations for Covid-19 without endanger human safety.

Vision

Provide tools that allow stakeholders to promptly act upon possible people infected with Covid-19. Thus promoting safety, protecting and drastically reducing Covid-19 spreading
